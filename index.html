Tetris




Tetris was created in 1984 by Alexey Pajitnov, an AI researcher working for the Soviet Academy of Sciences in Moscow. Tasked with testing the capabilities of new hardware, Pajitnov would do so by writing simple games for them. Tetris is one of my favorite games of all-time so I thought of honoring Alexey by recreating it for my first independent coding project.

Cache




People don't save because the returns banks give us suck. We created Cache as a way for people like us to earn a very high risk-free return, like interest from a bank savings account. Except that "interest" is money to spend on some great consumer partners. Our members can earn as much as 12% on each dollar they save over the course of a year! And we even give them 25% of those savings in cash back! Find out more and see how Cache works by clicking on the picture of Uncle Scrooge!

It was an honor to conceive and build this group project with my team: Cara Chew, Ian Chong, Vikas Garg, Bao Ling Koh.
